Frequently asked questions

FAQ

Who is TAGRA Trucker designed for?
For professional drivers — truck and van drivers — who want to keep track of their own card data and monitor compliance with driving times and rest periods.
Is the USB card reader included?
Yes. The USB driver card reader is included in the box. You can read your card immediately without buying anything extra.
How do I get data into the software?
Connect the included USB reader, insert your driver card, and TAGRA reads the data automatically. You can also import .DDD or .C1B files you've already downloaded elsewhere.
How often should I download my card?
As an operator or self-employed driver you are legally required to download driver card data at least every 28 days. Even as an employed driver, regular downloads help you stay on top of your hours and avoid unexpected violations.
Does TAGRA Trucker work offline?
Yes. TAGRA Trucker works fully offline. Internet is only needed to download the installer and receive updates.
Is TAGRA Trucker ready for the July 2026 van obligation?
Yes. TAGRA Trucker is fully prepared for van drivers (2.5–3.5 t vehicles in international transport) affected by the new tachograph obligation from July 2026.
How do I buy TAGRA Trucker after the trial?
At the end of the trial — or whenever you're ready — open the licence menu inside the software, select TAGRA Trucker and pay online by QR code or bank transfer. Your licence is activated immediately.
